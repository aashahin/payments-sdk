**Step 1.** Click on the **Authorised** order to be cancelled to go into its Order Details then click on the `Cancel` button.

**Step 2.** Enter the amount to be cancelled and a related comment for validating the cancellation, then click on the `Submit` button.

**Step 3.** Confirm the cancellation request by clicking on the `Cancel Order` button.
